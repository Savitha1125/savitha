About

Vegetables are a rich source of vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber, all of which contribute to overall health and well-being. They contain various essential nutrients like vitamins A, C, and K, potassium, calcium, and iron.Furthermore, vegetables are generally low in calories and fat, making them a healthy addition to any diet.Fruits and vegetables, like red peppers, are rich in vitamin C, with some containing over 190mg per serving. Consuming a variety of fruits and vegetables is linked to a reduced risk of chronic diseases, including heart disease, stroke, and certain cancers.
